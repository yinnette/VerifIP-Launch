VIP Insure™: Collaboration Solutions for Insurance

VerifIP® VIP Insure™ solutions provide streamlined business processes to share documents and store assets securely while simultaneously providing a portal for communication and feedback from customers and employees, allowing insurance firms to adapt to an ever changing environment, to more effectively deal with current policies and claims, and create new and sustainable intelligence driven products in a competitive marketplace.

Insurance firms depend on VerifIP® VIP Insure™ for:

Insurance Customer
“Self Service”
Personalize the user experience for your Policy Holders with VIP Insure; Customers can submit FNOL, access policy information, request quotes for new policies, submit payment, or chat with an agent. Enhance your customers experience by giving your Policy Holders the flexibility to securely connect how they want, when they want through web, social or mobile platforms. Mange all correspondence between you and your customer in one secure location.
Insurance Agent Portal
Create differentiation with your agent community with a single source for marketing, training, policy forms, and collaboration with your organization. Access though a central portal or on the go with mobile apps provides insurance agents policy documentation, marketing resources and training information at their fingertips. Provides your Marketing and Sales Department assurance that agents are accessing the most current policy information and materials.
Claims Process Optimization
Central depository for critical documents throughout each step of the claims process for all stakeholders. Reduction in Claim Cycle Time with a quicker FNOL, assignment of field adjuster and collaboration on a claim through a single-source. Claims Agents access to the network of partners with rich profiling of their skills, expertise and location to allow you to scale quickly.
New Product Development
Enable your Marketing and Product Development Team to hit the mark the first time with VIP Inusre. Collect insight into customer needs through consolidated feedback and social listening. Streamline Product Development through cross-departmental collaboration. Consolidate NAIC filing forms and documentation to step through the regulatory approval process more efficiently and increase your speed to market.
Compliance and Audit Management
Utilized as an Audit Management System, VIP Insure improves efficiency and reduces the cost of managing Underwriting Audits through an automated and collaborative platform. Create and store current Audit Documents in a central location for easy access. Upload large images and files quickly, without a concern about file size. Conduct on-site audits or invite external audit participants to submit information with user-defined permission access. Create new conversations and share feedback among the team around potential compliance or regulatory concerns.
Business Expertise Management
Manage Expertise. Contractor and Adjuster Profiles with Ratings. Integrates with Accounting System/W2s. Masks Secure Information based on User Profile.
